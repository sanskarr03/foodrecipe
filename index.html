Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Cooked chicken
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Mushrooms
  • Corn
  • Spinach
  • Boiled eggs
  • Seaweed
  • Toppings of your choice (sliced chilies, sesame seeds, pickled ginger, garlic, hot sauce)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pot, heat up the chicken or vegetable broth over medium heat. Bring it to a simmer and let it cook for a few minutes.
  3. While the broth is cooking, prepare the toppings. Chop the cooked chicken, green onions, and mushrooms. Rinse and drain the bean sprouts, bamboo shoots, and corn. Cut the seaweed into thin strips.
  4. Add the chopped chicken, bean sprouts, green onions, bamboo shoots, mushrooms, and corn to the broth. Let it simmer for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Divide the cooked ramen noodles among serving bowls.
  6. Ladle the hot broth and vegetables over the noodles.
  7. Cut the boiled eggs in half and add them to the bowls.
  8. Add the seaweed and any other toppings of your choice to the bowls.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious bowl of homemade ramen!
